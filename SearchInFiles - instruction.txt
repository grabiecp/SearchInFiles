How to fill in the parameters:
1. Path: enter a path from where you start searching. Have in mind that if you will enter c:\, script
will search in all subdirectories - it may take a while.

2. TextToFind: straitforward, just enter some text you expect to find inside the files.

3. FileType: enter extension of the files you are seeking. E.g pdf or xls (doesn't matter if you will
enter xls or xlsx, same for doc and docx and ppt pptx - script will search in excel word or powerpoint files).
Script can also search within all files that can be opened using notepad - txt of course but other types
as well may be included. Just enter the file extension.
